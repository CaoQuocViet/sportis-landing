Killer Features

The problems you face daily ‚Äî we've solved them

Easy Group Payment Splitting

One person books the court, the system auto-generates a payment link to share with the group. Each person pays their share via Momo, ZaloPay, or bank transfer ‚Äî no account creation, no app install needed.

Solves: Completely eliminates chasing each person for money after every game.

15-Minute Secure Hold

When you select a time slot, the system exclusively holds it for you for 15 minutes to complete payment. No one can "jump the queue" during this time.

Solves: No more losing slots because someone else paid a few seconds faster.

Recurring Booking, Flexible Cancellation

Book courts on a weekly basis (e.g., every Tuesday at 7 PM) but still cancel individual sessions without affecting the remaining bookings.

Solves: Most other platforms force "cancel all or keep all" ‚Äî not practical for real schedules.

Find Players at Your Level

Skill rating system on a 0-7 scale, verified through actual match results. Find opponents or teammates that match your ability.

Solves: Avoids "Intermediate" players facing "Beginners" ‚Äî ruining the experience for both sides.

Fair No-Show Policy

If you miss a booked session, 30% of the penalty fee is converted to credit for your next booking.

Solves: Protects venue owner revenue while retaining players instead of pushing them to other platforms.

Sports Community

Connect with players who share your interests, share experiences, and book courts together. Communicate directly with venue owners, get support from the platform.

Solves: Building a sports network ‚Äî not just booking courts, but creating lasting connections.
